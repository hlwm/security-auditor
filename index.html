ℹ How this dashboard works
📥
Step 1 — Download WS1/SCCM reports
Each month, download the software inventory Excel files from Workspace ONE (WS1) and SCCM for every office. Each file must contain sheets: SCCM-WinOS, WS1-WinOS, WS1-MacOS.
📤
Step 2 — Upload all files at once
Drag & drop or click to select all office Excel files together. The dashboard automatically merges, deduplicates by serial number, and classifies each device by office and department.
🔬
Step 3 — Add Software Tracker (optional)
For scientific software compliance, go to Sci SW & Licensing and upload your Licensed SW Excel from the SharePoint Software Tracker. Matched by Staff ID + software name.

⚠ Over-Licensed Software — Who are the excess users?

Users are sorted by office. Rows highlighted in red exceed the licensed seat count — these are the excess installs that need to be addressed. ⓘ What do the statuses mean?
✓
MATCH
Device detected in WS1/SCCM AND found in Licensed SW inventory for the same office & software. License is accounted for.
🚨
UNLICENSED
Device detected in WS1/SCCM scan BUT there is no record in your Licensed SW inventory. Software is installed but not registered — potential compliance risk.
👻
GHOST
Record exists in Licensed SW inventory BUT device was never detected in WS1/SCCM. May be uninstalled, device retired, or wrong office in records.
⏰
EXPIRED
Found in both sources (MATCH) BUT the license period end date in your inventory has already passed. Renewal required.
